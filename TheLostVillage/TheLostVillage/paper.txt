You have Five Days to find The Lost Village where people will be able to help the Hungarians Defeat the Turks. 
1) Get through the Cursed Forest  
2) Get experience in fighting by killing Monsters/Foes
3) Find villagers after the Forest and ask for Items
4) Move on to the Foggy Mountain
5) Play a game with the Wizzard and win to claim your prize
6) Defeat the Guardian of the Mountain
7)Find TheLostVillage
